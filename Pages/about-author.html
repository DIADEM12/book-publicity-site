About the Author

Oluwafemi Odesola is a dynamic scholar, writer, and sustainable development practitioner with a deep passion for faith, research, and sociopolitical engagement. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Agricultural Economics and a Master’s in Sustainable Development from the University of Ibadan, Nigeria. Now based in the United Kingdom, he integrates academic depth with purposeful writing, specializing in research, citations, biographies, and sociopolitical analysis.

A devoted disciple of Christ, Oluwafemi has enjoyed 18 years of close spiritual mentorship under Rev’d Dr. John Akin Oyelade, serving as both his son in the Lord and interpreter. This enduring relationship has significantly shaped his theological outlook and inspired much of his writing.

As a thoughtful and engaged voice, he has presented research papers at international conferences in South Africa and the United States, contributing to global conversations on sustainable development and faith-informed approaches to societal challenges. Beyond his intellectual and spiritual endeavors, Oluwafemi is a loving husband to Mary Mercy. Together, they make their home in the UK.

This book embodies his distinctive fusion of scholarly rigor, spiritual depth, and literary passion written to inspire, educate, and stir meaningful reflection.
